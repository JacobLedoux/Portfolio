Auto printfarm

Oct 2023 - ​June 2024

This project served as a capstone for Essential Skills. In essence, the project is a 3D printer farm that has been fully automated. The goal was to simplify 3D printing for students, making it as easy as filling out a form.  I made my progress open source.

InMoov

Oct 2021 - ​May 2023

InMoov is a life sized fully 3D-printed humanoid robot. It is an open-sourced project I started in 10th grade alongside my regular courses. I learned a lot from this project and it became a great stepping stone for more difficult tasks I had coming throughout my high school career.

GigaPrint

Dec 2023 - ​June 2024

Gigaprint is essentially just a larger delta printer. It has a print volume of 500mm x 500mm x 940mm. It is a project I took on upon the graduation of a student. This project will be open-sourced upon completion although progress is updated regularly.
